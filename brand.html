Vertigo House

A brand created by creatives, for creatives. If we have one goal, it is to change the landscape of clothing design with our own fun, unique, and intriguing spin on graphics.

Our Story

Vertigo House was born out of a passion for both fashion and individuality. Founded by me (VERTIGO222) and my twin brother (SLEEPYHOUSE), our brand reflects our commitment to innovation and style. We wanted to create a clothing line that empowers people to express themselves and embrace their unique identity. As lifelong artists and fashion lovers, we always felt gravitated towards creating clothes. It wasnt until we began our journey with graphic design, however, that we finally had the means to make this dream a reality- and that's just what we did. While we are still small, we hope to inspire others with similar dreams and we are beyond grateful to be able to follow our dreams in such a way.

Our Vision

At Vertigo House, we strive to combine quality, creativity, and culture in every piece we create. Our designs are inspired by a blend of urban trends, skate culture, streetwear, and timeless styles. Each piece is curated for those who dare to stand out. Join us as we push the boundaries of fashion and redefine what it means to look and feel good. Eventually, we hope to see Vertigo House being represented worldwide, by many likeminded individuals.

Our New Collection

We are excited to introduce our latest collection. Each piece has been designed with attention to detail and a bit more simplistic approach to our previous collections. That said, we hope that this collection has something for everyone!
